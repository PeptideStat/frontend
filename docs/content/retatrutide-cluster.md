## Compliance & E-E-A-T notes (read before writing anything)

Retatrutide is a **YMYL (Your Money or Your Life) health topic** and is **not
FDA approved** — Google holds this content to a high trust bar. Every article
must:

- Open with, or prominently include, the **medical + investigational-status
  disclaimer** (the `<Disclaimer />` component already does this on-page).
- **Cite primary sources** — the NEJM Phase 2 trial, PMC meta-analyses, the
  Eli Lilly TRIUMPH program — not other blogs.
- Frame human-use claims carefully: retatrutide **cannot be legally sold for
  human use**; "research use" framing only on commercial pages.
- Show **author + review** info (E-E-A-T). Needs a real author bio + an
  author page — flagged as a site-level to-do.
- Be **dated and "last reviewed"** — Phase 3 TRIUMPH data is landing through
  2026, so these articles will need refreshes.

## Minoxidil Layer

Minoxidil is not a peptide product, but it belongs in the protocol because it
has direct human evidence for androgenetic alopecia. Blueprint's public protocol
mentions topical minoxidil and oral minoxidil, but those are different risk
categories.

  <thead>

      <th>Role</th>
      <th>Notes</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Topical minoxidil</td>
      <td>Evidence-backed hair-growth medication layer.</td>
      <td>Use according to the label or a prescriber's instructions. Let it dry before layering other scalp products.</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Prescription topical blends</td>
      <td>Clinician-directed formulas may combine minoxidil with other actives.</td>

## Safety Rules

Patch test the shampoo and serum first. Stop if you get burning, rash, severe
itching, or unusual shedding. Be careful with irritated, infected, painful,
sunburned, or recently treated scalps.

See a dermatologist for sudden shedding, patchy hair loss, scalp pain,
scarring, infection, or hair loss tied to a medication or medical condition.
For oral minoxidil, prescription minoxidil blends, finasteride, or dutasteride,
use clinician guidance.

## FAQ

### What is Bryan Johnson's hair protocol?

Bryan Johnson's public hair protocol combines scalp products, hair-growth
medications, and device-based light therapy. This page focuses on the practical
stack people usually mean when they search for the Bryan Johnson hair protocol:
Blueprint Peptide Shampoo, Blueprint Peptide Hair Serum, the 302 Laser Cap, and
minoxidil.

### What products are in the Bryan Johnson hair protocol?

The core Blueprint hair products are Peptide Shampoo, Peptide Hair Serum, and
the 302 Laser Cap. The broader protocol also references minoxidil as a
hair-growth medication layer.

### Does Bryan Johnson use minoxidil?

Bryan Johnson's public protocol references both topical minoxidil and oral
minoxidil. Topical minoxidil is available over the counter in many markets.
Oral minoxidil is prescription and off-label for hair loss, so it needs
clinician guidance.

### Does Blueprint Peptide Hair Serum replace minoxidil?

No. Blueprint's peptide shampoo and serum are cosmetic scalp products. They can
fit next to minoxidil in a routine, but they should not be treated as direct
replacements for proven hair-loss medications.

### How often do you use the Blueprint 302 Laser Cap?

Blueprint's laser cap directions describe daily use on dry hair and scalp for 6
minutes. Consistency matters more than occasional long sessions.

### Which matters more, the shampoo or the serum?

The serum is the stronger peptide step because it stays on the scalp. The
shampoo can still be useful as scalp prep, but rinse-off products have less
contact time.

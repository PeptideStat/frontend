Ozempic and Wegovy are often treated like interchangeable names, but that is
not accurate. They both contain semaglutide, but they are separate products
with different labels, dose targets and insurance logic.

The short answer: **Wegovy is the semaglutide weight-loss brand. Ozempic is the
semaglutide type 2 diabetes brand.** Both can affect weight, but the label
matters.

## Same Molecule, Different Product

The active drug is semaglutide in both. The difference is how the product is
labeled, titrated, prescribed, covered and studied.

Ozempic is built around type 2 diabetes treatment and glycemic control. Wegovy
is built around chronic weight management in eligible adults and adolescents,
with additional cardiometabolic label context.

That is why "Ozempic vs Wegovy" is usually not a chemistry question. It is a
regulatory and practical-use question.

## Side Effects

Because both contain semaglutide, the side-effect pattern overlaps:

- Nausea.
- Vomiting.
- Diarrhea.
- Constipation.
- Reflux or abdominal discomfort.
- Reduced appetite.
- Injection-site reactions for injectable products.

Dose, titration and patient factors matter. The same person may tolerate one
semaglutide dose poorly and another better.

## Bottom Line

Ozempic and Wegovy are not two different peptides. They are two semaglutide
brands aimed at different clinical uses. If the goal is weight management,
Wegovy is the cleaner label match. If the goal is type 2 diabetes treatment,
Ozempic is the cleaner label match.

## FAQ

### Are Ozempic and Wegovy the same drug?

They contain the same active ingredient, semaglutide, but they are different
branded products with different labels, dose targets and indications.

### Which is better for weight loss, Ozempic or Wegovy?

Wegovy is the semaglutide brand labeled for chronic weight management. Ozempic
can cause weight loss, but its main label is type 2 diabetes.

### Why does Wegovy usually use higher semaglutide dosing?

The weight-management label was developed around obesity trial dosing, while
Ozempic was developed primarily for glycemic control in type 2 diabetes.

### Can Ozempic be used for weight loss?

Ozempic is sometimes used off label for weight loss, but Wegovy is the
semaglutide product with a weight-management label.

### Do Ozempic and Wegovy have the same side effects?

They share semaglutide-related GLP-1 side effects, especially gastrointestinal
symptoms. Dose and patient factors change the experience.
